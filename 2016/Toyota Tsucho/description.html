Toyota Tsusho Electronics (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (TTET) was established in April 2005 as a 

joint venture between Toyota Tsusho Electronics Co., Ltd. and Toyota Tsusho Group, with the 

aim of developing Embedded Software for Automotive Applications.

Embedded Software for Automotive Applications calls for a high degree of reliability because of 

the high level of functionality and large scale applications.

As a member of Japanese car manufacturing companies, TTET continually trains its embedded 

software development engineers in topics such as programming languages, software develop-
ment processes and quality assurance to ensure that the end product is of high quality, delivered 

on time and within cost. We also aim to contribute to the economy of Thailand and its industries.
